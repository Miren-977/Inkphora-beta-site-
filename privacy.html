Privacy

Last updated: 2025-09-14

Who we are

Inkphora is developing an empathic AI experience powered by the Thymiko prototype. This page explains how we handle your data during the beta trial.

What we collect

  • Waitlist: your email address to contact you about beta access and updates (double opt‑in will be enabled when our provider is connected).
  • Prototype sessions (V1/V2): anonymous stroke features and brief self‑reports of mood. We do not store raw drawings by default.
  • No ads, no third‑party trackers.

Legal basis & purposes

Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). To provide the beta experience, improve the model in aggregate, and notify about important changes or safety info.

Retention

  • Waitlist: until you unsubscribe or up to 12 months after beta.
  • Anonymous session data: retained for improvement; deletion available if reasonably re‑identifiable.

Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent; complaint to your authority.
