# Beta Guide — Inkphora

Inkphora is currently in private beta.

The current prototype is called Thymiko.

It operates using the Inkphora Gesture-Affect Engine™, our proprietary gesture-based affective interpretation framework.

The current prototype (Thymiko) is optimized for mobile touchscreen interaction and gesture input.

---

## What “beta” means

- Early experimental access  
- Features may change  
- Interface may evolve  
- Feedback improves the system  

This is a research prototype.

It is NOT:  
- a finished commercial product  
- a therapy service  
- a medical or diagnostic tool  

---

## How to use the beta

Each session consists of a short freehand drawing.

You can use the app 1–2 times per day.

There is no need to draw anything specific.  
Spontaneous gestures work best.

Each interaction is brief and designed to capture your natural movement.

---

## Duration of the beta

This is an initial testing phase.

The duration may evolve based on results.

---

## Why results may not always feel accurate

The system does not detect emotions as facts.

It interprets patterns in movement and translates them into a possible emotional direction.

Sometimes the result may resonate.  
Sometimes it may not.

This variability is part of the validation process.

---

## What this system is (and is not)

Inkphora does not detect emotions as objective truths.

It does not provide psychological or medical evaluation.

It generates poetic reflections and musical suggestions based on gesture dynamics.

---

## Do I need to register

No registration is required to use the prototype.

---

## Data and privacy

No personal identity is stored.  
No name or personal profile is required.

The system does not use biometric data such as face or voice.

Only gesture-related features (such as speed, rhythm, and movement patterns) are processed.

Sessions may be technically linked to allow consistency across interactions.

This is used only for data normalization and system improvement.  
This linkage is not connected to personal identity.

No drawings are stored as images.

---

## What is stored

- gesture features (numerical data)  
- optional self-assessment inputs  
- generated outputs (text)  

---

## Why this music is suggested

The system does not generate only music.

Each interaction produces a poetic reflection and a set of music suggestions.

Music is selected to reflect the general emotional direction inferred from your gesture.

Different styles may be suggested, including classical, ragas, or ambient sound.

Music is only one part of the experience, not the main output.

---

## What to do during the beta

Use the app naturally across multiple sessions.

There is no correct way to interact.

Your repeated use helps validate how gesture patterns behave over time.
