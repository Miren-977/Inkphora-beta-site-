## What is Thymiko?

Thymiko is the first working prototype of Inkphora.
It allows users to draw simple strokes and receive symbolic interpretations and music suggestions.

---

## Is this therapy?

No.
Inkphora is not a medical, psychological, or diagnostic tool.
It is a research prototype focused on affective interaction.

---

## Do I need an account?

No account is required for the prototype.
Email is collected only for beta communication.

---

## What data is collected?

We may collect:
- email (if voluntarily submitted)
- anonymous gesture-derived features
- optional short mood self-reports

We do not collect biometric data.
