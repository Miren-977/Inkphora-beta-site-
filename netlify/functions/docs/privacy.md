# Privacy & Data Handling

Inkphora is designed to minimise data collection.

## What We Collect

### 1. Waitlist

- Email address (voluntarily submitted)

Used only for beta communication.

### 2. Prototype Sessions

We may store:

- Anonymous gesture-derived features
- Optional short self-reports (if provided)

We do NOT store:
- raw biometric data
- camera or audio recordings
- advertising identifiers

Raw drawings are not stored by default in early versions.

---

## Legal Basis

Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)).

Data is used only to:
- provide beta access
- improve the affective interpretation model in aggregate
- ensure system reliability

---

## Retention

- Email: until unsubscribe or up to 12 months after beta
- Anonymous session data: retained for research validation

If data becomes reasonably re-identifiable, deletion is possible upon request.
